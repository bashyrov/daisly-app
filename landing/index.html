Where do subscriptions happen?

Subscriptions are handled through Apple in-app purchases. Your iPhone will show the final localized price before purchase.

Does Daisly edit my calendar automatically?

No. Daisly only uses connected calendar and task access to sync the planning features you choose to enable.
